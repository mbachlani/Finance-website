Insurance Protection

Practical protection and financial education to help you and your family stay secure through life’s changes.

Life Insurance

Provides a financial safety net for your loved ones in the event of death. Options include term and permanent policies tailored to your family's needs and budget.

Critical Illness Insurance

One-time lump-sum benefit if you're diagnosed with a covered critical illness. Designed to cover medical expenses, mortgage or living costs while you recover.

Disability Coverage

Income protection if you become unable to work due to injury or illness. Keeps bills paid and preserves long-term financial plans.

Health & Dental Plans

Supplemental coverage for prescriptions, dental care, paramedical services and more — helps reduce out-of-pocket healthcare costs.

Visitors Insurance

Short-term medical coverage for visitors to Canada. Covers emergency care, hospital stays and unexpected medical expenses while visiting.
